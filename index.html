Hi, I’m Zewei

I come from Xining, Qinghai Province located mostly on the Tibetan Plateau. The Chinese name "Qinghai" is after Qinghai Lake, the largest lake in China. The lake is known as Kokonor Lake in English, derived from the Mongol Oirat name for Qinghai Lake.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Business Analytics at Lindner College of Business in the University of Cincinnati. Before joining UC, I obtained BS in Statistics, BA in Philosophy, and BEcon in Economic Statistics from Renmin University of China in 2019. CV Link
